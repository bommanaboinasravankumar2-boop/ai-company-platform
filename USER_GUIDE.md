# Synapse OS User Guide

Welcome to **Synapse OS**! This guide is designed to help business operations managers, developers, and founders master our unified multi-agent environment and outbound sales dashboards.

---

## 🖥️ Executive Hub Overview

The Synapse OS homepage compiles real-time corporate metrics:
1. **Financial Counters:** Active subscriber statistics, pipeline values, and cumulative recurring revenue (MRR).
2. **System Log Watcher:** Keeps audit logs on all manual database additions, CRM scans, and AI Generations.

---

## 🤖 Working with the 13 Specialized AI Agents

Synapse OS hosts 13 distinct agent personas built upon the Gemini 3.5 AI engine. To initiate chats:
1. Select the **AI Co-Pilots** tab.
2. Choose an agent from the visual selector grid (e.g., *Sophia (CFO)* for business financials, or *Leo (Legal)* for commercial contracts).
3. Draft your prompt in the message bar and click submit.
4. Use the custom sidebar control panels to reset histories, copy recommendations, or export outputs.

---

## 📊 CRM & Interactive Screenshot Auditor

The CRM contains a built-in search pipeline for qualified leads. It has been upgraded with an interactive screenshot browser:

### Exploring Lead Details
* Click on any lead card in the left list (e.g., *Veloce Logistics* or *Bharat Healthtech*).
* In the main detail window, toggle between **Executive Summary** and **Interactive Audit Screenshot**.

### Using the Visual Screenshot Audit
* Click the **Interactive Audit Screenshot** tab.
* You will see a simulated desktop browser showcasing the client's current landing page.
* **Gaps Inspection:** Locate and hover/click any glowing red `!` hotspots. A detailed overlay card will appear explaining:
  * The performance block or missing feature.
  * Estimated annual business loss due to this deficiency.
  * A step-by-step mitigation strategy.
* **Simulating the Upgrade:** Toggle the **Simulation Switch** in the top right. Watch the website immediately optimize:
  * Page Speed score jumps to 98/100 (emerald indicator).
  * Glowing spots turn to emerald checkmarks.
  * An active **AURA customer chat widget** floats in.
  * Open the chat widget to test interactive conversations with the client's virtual support agent!

## 📧 Outbound Autopilots

Under the lead dashboard, access quick generation tool buttons:
* **Draft Proposal:** Generates complete customized pricing, timelines, and ROIs.
* **Write Email:** Drafts personalized email campaigns customized in regional languages.
* **Schedule Sync:** Books placeholder events synced directly to calendar lists.
